A game for all

What is domi ?

A nft version of the traditional game

Domi is the traditional domino game where the 2v2 game mode is won by adding the points obtained in each round until reaching 100 points, throughout the game each player places a domino piece on the table in an organized way, and the first player to run out of pieces gives victory to his team. We do not intend or want to change the way the traditional domino is played, our vision is to implement it in this new era of nft games so that by playing this entertaining game you can earn income.

Roadmap investment

Phase 1 - seed investors

Investors who enter this phase will be able to obtain a profit of 10% of the commissions obtained in the market and 10% obtained from the $ DOMI tokens obtained from the improvement of categories and cloning. Investors in this phase will also enjoy exclusive content of all the progress of the project, as well as being part of the test team of our demos. This investment round will be available for a limited time.

The funds obtained in this phase will be used to:
Project development
Resources
Marketing
Development template expansion

Phase 2 - Domino pieces set sale

In this phase, the first sets of domino pieces will be sold, investors will be able to choose between the different rarities of sets that exist in the game, taking into account that the higher the investment you will get a better set of pieces and a higher profit.

The funds obtained in this phase will be used to:
Project development
Resources
Marketing
$DRW liquidity pool

Phase 3 - sale of tables

In this phase, investors will be able to buy tables of the game, where they will obtain a profit from the players for using their tables.
This investment phase will have a time limit, the tables that have not been sold at the end of the round will be taken by the team for the benefit of the project.

The funds obtained in this phase will be used to:
Resources
$DRW liquidity pool
$DOMI liquidity pool

Phase 4 - $DOMI token presale

In this phase, investors will buy $DOMI tokens, which will be priced at 0.10$ for each token, the minimum to invest is 200$ and the maximum is 25,000$. In addition to receiving tokens, investors will receive an nft of a set of domino pieces based on their investment.
The amount of tokens to be sold in this phase is 1,200,000.

  • Investment greater than 200$ will receive a Bone domino set
  • Investment greater than 300$ will receive a wooden domino set
  • Investment greater than 600$ will receive a Bronze domino set
  • Investment greater than 1000$ will receive a Silver domino set
  • Investment greater than 3000$ will receive a Gold domino set
The funds obtained in this phase will be used to:
Project development
Resources
Marketing
Liquidity pool
